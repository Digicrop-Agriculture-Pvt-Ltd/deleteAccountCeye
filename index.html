Account Deletion – CEYE DIGICROP

CEYE DIGICROP allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request account deletion

Please email us at sales@digi-crop.com with the following details:

  • Registered mobile number
  • Subject: "Delete My CEYE DIGICROP Account"

What data is deleted

  • User account information (mobile number)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Associated user data stored in our systems

If you have any questions, contact us at sales@digi-crop.com.
