Account Deletion – CEYE DIGICROP

CEYE DIGICROP respects user privacy and provides a simple way to request deletion of your account and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email to:

sales@digi-crop.com

Include the following information:

  • Registered mobile number
  • Subject line: Delete My CEYE DIGICROP Account

What Data Is Deleted

  • User account information (mobile number)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Associated user data stored in our systems

Once your request is verified, all applicable personal data will be permanently deleted in accordance with our data retention policies.

For any questions regarding data privacy or account deletion, contact us at sales@digi-crop.com
